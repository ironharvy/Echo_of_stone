## Why Alexei Builds the Robot

**Surface**: Thesis project. Art + engineering. Impressive.

**Practical**: Consuming enough to justify university existence, which keeps him from military.

**Emotional (unconscious)**: A figure that's beautiful but can't reject him. Company without risk. Safety.

**Technical**: He's genuinely good at this. The problem is interesting.

**The two Alexeis' reasons diverge**:
- Nerd Alexei: The engineering puzzle. Elegance of solution.
- Artist Alexei: The form. The beauty. The Venus. What does it mean to create a body?

Neither knows the other's motivation. Together, they make something neither would make alone.

## Alexei's Strange Habit

**Only Nerd Alexei** has this: cannot stand drawers left slightly open. Closes them compulsively.

**Usage**:
- Dmitri leaves drawer open. Alexei closes it silently. Repeatedly.
- Eventually Dmitri notices: "You always do that."
- Alexei: "Do what?"

**Split personality tell**: When drawers are left open in his workspace, we know who was there last. Subtle, for rewatch.

## Scene Sequence

### PROLOGUE: JULIA

The rejection. Core wound. Brief, painful, formative.

**Setting**: School dance, party, or after class. Alexei finally acts.

**Key line**: "I always thought you weren't interested."

She's not cruel. She's moved on, or never saw him that way because he never showed her.

**Aftermath**: Alexei alone. Processing. Maybe sees an easy couple together.

**Internal realization**: *If I don't impose myself, I don't exist.*

**Pivot to robot (subtle, for rewatch)**: Later, a glimpse of Alexei looking at classical sculpture. Venus. Idealized female form. Beautiful, silent, *cannot reject him*. The robot isn't just art. It's safety.
