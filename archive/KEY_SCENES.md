## Confirmed Scenes

### 1. The God Almighty Scene (Season 1)

**Setup**: Sergei's guest lecture at Dubna. Alexei and Dmitri attend, slightly drunk.

**The exchange**:
> DMITRI: If God is almighty, can he create a stone he himself can't lift?
> SERGEI: (confused) Of course he can.
> DMITRI: Then he's not almighty, because there's a stone he can't lift.

**What it establishes**:
- Sergei's intellectual framework before we know what he's building
- The theme of systems that contradict themselves
- Dmitri's character (irreverent, pokes at authority)
- Alexei's position (present but quiet, observing)

**Deeper resonance**: Sergei *is* trying to be God â€” creating life, defeating death. He will succeed in a way that proves his limitations. Elena-in-the-statue is the stone he cannot lift: his daughter, but now beyond his control.

### 3. The Mirror Scenes â€” Two Scenes That Rhyme (Seasons 1 & 2)

**Scene A: Alexei and the Mirror (Season 1)**

**Scene B: Elena and the Mirror (Season 2)**

Elena, newly in the statue, asks for a mirror. She wants to see herself.

She sees:
- A face she didn't choose
- Beauty that isn't hers â€” it's his idea of beauty
- Classical proportions, idealized, *made*
- No trace of her biological face

She touches her face while looking. The disconnect between proprioception and vision.

She might ask Alexei why he made the face this way. What was he imagining? He can't fully answer â€” *he* didn't make all of it.

**The question**: If your face is someone else's creation, someone else's fantasy, do you inhabit it or wear it? Can you own a face you didn't grow into?

**The Rhyme**:
| Alexei | Elena |
|--------|-------|

**Staging option**: Elena sees Alexei do his mirror-check after she's in the statue. She recognizes the hesitation, the searching â€” because she just experienced her own version. This is how she first suspects his duality.

### 6. The Diagnosis Scene (Season 2)

**Structure**:
- Elena gets news alone. Doctor is professional. For her, time stops.
- She doesn't cry. She doesn't process. She receives.
- The gap: she leaves clinic, sits somewhere, stares at nothing.
- She goes to Sergei. Says it simply. Too simply.

> ELENA: I saw Dr. [name] today.
> SERGEI: (not looking up) And?
> ELENA: (beat) It's not good.

He looks up. His face is where we see the blow land. His scientist brain calculates the timeline. His father heart breaks.

She watches him understand. In watching *him* react, she finally starts to feel it herself.
